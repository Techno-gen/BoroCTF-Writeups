Summary
This one was fun. The clue says: "My soul is empty, my name too. 1228 is where I lay, yet I keep on pushing." The key was not a normal user profile. 1228 pointed to a CTFd team page whose name was the empty-set symbol. That team linked to an X/Twitter profile, and its public solve list hid the message.

Initially, I started out thinking about Satoshi Nakamoto, since I assumed this challenge was completely unrelated. After I had exhausted all possible leads relating to the blockchain and block height 1228, I began probing the internet for key phrases like "1228," "Satoshi," and "soul." As a final hail mary, I transitioned to probing the boroCTF site itself.

I eventually landed on this page:
https://boroctf.com/teams/1228

This team had a ∅(empty-set, hence "empty soul") symbol name, and, by some miracle, led back to the https://x.com/SatoshiNakamuda page from the earlier Satoshi challenges created by ForeverFlames.
