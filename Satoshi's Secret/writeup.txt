Summary
If Satoshi Hunt wasn't hard enough, this one kicks it up a notch. To find the flag, you have to go to a neighboring user profile @soulfullybinded that interacted with Satoshi cryptically, and examine the gemini-generated profile picture for a date. This date will bring you to the Dyatlov Pass Incident.

In his few posts, Satoshi has also interacted with another account by the name of @soulfullybinded at https://x.com/soulfullybinded. Soulbinded posted an image containing Brainfuck code, which will come into play later. Satoshi responds with 
Factum est. Nunc me in pace relinquite.
Which is basically along the lines of "It is done. Now leave me in peace."

If you go to soulbinded's profile, among his cryptic bio(which will also be important later) you will see his profile picture of some cryptid in a forest. If you download the image and look up the date in the bottom left corner, you will find the incident.
